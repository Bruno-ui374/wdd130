Mukono, Uganda

Mukono is a town in the Central Region of Uganda. It is located about 21 kilometers east of Kampala, the capital city of Uganda. Mukono is known for its vibrant community, educational institutions, and beautiful landscapes. The town serves as a commercial center for the surrounding rural areas and is home to several industries and businesses.
